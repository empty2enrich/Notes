# e

相关知识：黎曼和估算，麦克劳林展开
$$
e = \lim_{x \to ∞}(1 + \frac{1}{x})^x \\
e = \lim_{x \to 0}x^0 + ... + \frac{1}{k!}x^k + .... \\
e^r = \lim_{x \to ∞}(1 + \frac{r}{x})^x \\
\ln x = \int_{0 \to ∞} \frac{1}{x}d(x) \\
(\ln ax)' = \frac{1}{x}\\
\ln 1 = \ln ({a * \frac{1}{a}})  = \ln {a} + \ln{\frac{1}{a}} = 0
$$
